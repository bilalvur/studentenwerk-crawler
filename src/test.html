Offers

At the bottom of this page you will find an overview of the current offers of our Private Accommodation Service.

In order to obtain the contact details of the landlord/landlady, you need to register. Any questions? You might find the answers in the  Private Accommodation FAQs.

What's it all about?

We are pleased to have the opportunity to try and help you solve your accommodation problems in the Munich area by providing you with up-to-date information on available private rooms. Our Private Accommodation Service can, however, only be used by students and is only free of charge for students studying at one of the universities under the jurisdiction of the Munich Student Union!  For more information, go to the detailed list below (click on the offer ID).

How does it work?

Below, you'll find an overview of the latest offers, which can be sorted by different criteria (e.g. date, part of the city, rent etc.). Simply click on an offer ID to obtain more detailed information about that room or apartment.

If you are interested in a particular offer, please make a note of the 6-digit offer ID. If you already have a username, you can find out the contact details online. If you don’t have a username yet, you first need to register with the Private Accommodation Service.

  • Register
  • Find out contact details

Any questions?

Should you have any more questions about our Private Accommodation Service, you might find an answer in our Private Accommodation FAQs.

Important information:

If the data provided in an offer turns out not to be true or if you find out an offer is untrustworthy, we kindly ask you to contact us. For this purpose, please use the feedback forms or send an email to pzv @stwm.de .

Also, we advise you not to pay the landlord/landlady in cash. But if you do, we strongly advise you to insist on a receipt.
